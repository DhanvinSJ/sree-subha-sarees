Saree Pico
Quick Turnaround
$15
Clean, precise pico edging on your saree border for a neat, finished look that holds beautifully and lasts long.
Book Now →
Service Description

Saree pico is a delicate edging technique applied along the border of your saree to give it a clean, polished, and professional finish. It prevents fraying, adds a refined look, and makes your saree easier to drape and maintain.

Whether it's a new saree that needs finishing or an old favourite that needs care, we handle every saree with precision and attention to detail. Quick turnaround available.

Saree Falls
Expert Finishing
$25
Professionally stitched saree falls to give your saree the perfect weight, drape, and shape it deserves every time.
Book Now →
Service Description

A saree fall is a lining stitched along the bottom edge of your saree that gives it structure, weight, and a beautiful drape. It helps the saree fall gracefully, prevents the border from folding, and makes draping much easier and neater.

We stitch falls in matching or complementary colours to perfectly suit your saree. Ideal for both new sarees and older ones that need a refresh. Drop off your saree and we'll have it ready for you quickly.
